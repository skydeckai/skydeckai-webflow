Revolución de IA: Mantenga su negocio a la vanguardia, de manera segura.

Introducción:

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has transitioned from a futuristic concept to an essential tool for a modern business. However, as businesses rush to embrace this technology, they face a significant challenge: how to deploy advanced AI tools securely and responsibly.

Entendiendo el Desafío

Generative AI is no longer a futuristic concept; it’s a current reality. Many companies want to equip their teams with these powerful tools. However, deploying these Generative AI tools securely and with manageable risk to every employee in a business is complex. Due to these complexities, instead of leveraging this technology, the response for many organizations has been to outright ban generative AI.

“More than 60% of organisations in the UK have either banned, or are considering banning, the use of generative AI tools on employee- or business-owned devices”
